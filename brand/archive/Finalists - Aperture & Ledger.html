Where I'd land

LEADING · APERTURE

Framed facet

square 4.0 · diamond 2.6 · core 3.0 · radius 4.5

Giving the frame ~1.4× the weight of the facet resolves the busy crossings of the equal-weight original — the eye reads a confident container first, then notices the facet inside it. It holds a clean silhouette at 16px, and the lighter inner diamond keeps the wordmark lockup from feeling top-heavy. Emerald core stays the single warm note; the violet option is there if we want to break from the in-app "progress" green.
