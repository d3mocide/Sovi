Sovi · Brand Guidelines

Sovi

Your numbers. Your stack. Your control.

Sovi is a local-first debt & budgeting tool that runs on your own hardware — no cloud, no tracking, nothing leaving your network. This is the brand: calm, credible, private by design. A precise instrument you trust.

01

Brand essence

Sovi gives people full control over their financial data through a clean, local-first tool that never leaves their hardware. Its emotional thesis: clarity without guilt. Debt is framed as forward progress — never alarm.

Mission

Give people full control over their financial data through a clean, local-first tool that never leaves their hardware.

Personality

Calm, credible, modern. Slightly elegant and minimal. Tech-forward but not intimidating — friendly to individuals, never corporate.

Feeling

Closer to a well-made terminal or trading panel than a consumer fintech app — but warmer, and more legible.

Privacy by design Ownership Calm clarity Power without complexity Transparency
02

The mark

A square frame interlocked with a rotated diamond around an emerald core — an abstract aperture that reads as containment and ownership: your data, held. The facet carries the weight over a delicate frame; the emerald core is the single owned / settled note.

Primary — mark on tile

  • Framesquare, stroke 1.5px · radius 3px
  • Facetdiamond, stroke 2.5px
  • Coreemerald dot, r 2.6
  • Tilenavy #0f172a · radius 16
  • Strokessky #38bdf8

Construction & rules

  • Grid64×64 viewBox; mark inset to a 40px square, centered on both axes.
  • Clearspacethe mark's full height on all four sides — keep it uncrowded.
  • Min size16px — favicon-tested; below this the core fills in.
  • Core coloremerald #34d399. May shift to violet #a78bfa only to differentiate from the in-app "progress" green.
  • Wordmark"Sovi" in Inter 700, tracking -0.02em, set as live text.

  • Swap the core to emerald and a progress bar in the same view.
03

Color

A deep navy spine, one confident sky accent, emerald for progress, amber for the rare nudge. Built on Tailwind's slate + sky scales. Always reference the semantic alias in code, never the raw scale value.

Navy / Slate — the neutral spine

Sky — brand & action--accent · #38bdf8 · primary buttons (dark text on sky)
Emerald — forward progress--positive · #34d399 · "paid", streaks, the mark's core
Amber — attention--attention · #fbbf24 · the strongest signal we allow
Violet — secondary data--data-4 · #a78bfa · charts, simulator B series
No alarm red — anywhere. Debt going down is the story. Even the "danger" button is amber. Tints are the accent at ~15% opacity with a ~30% border; depth comes from surface contrast, never heavy color.

05

Voice

Calm, plain, second-person. Sovi talks to you — a quiet, competent companion, never a salesperson, never a scold. State the fact, skip the adjectives. The product's whole job is to remove anxiety from money.

We say
  • Debt-free in 14 months.
  • Your numbers. Your stack. Your control.
  • Sync queued — data will update shortly.
  • Add a debt to see your payoff date.
Not
  • You still owe a frightening $18,240!
  • Bank-grade AI-powered cloud finance.
  • ERROR: refresh failed!!!
  • You haven't set anything up yet.
Person. Second person — "you", "your". First-person plural only in marketing voice.
Casing. Sentence case everywhere. UPPERCASE only for tracked eyebrow labels. Title Case is avoided.
No guilt, no alarm. Never "you owe", "warning!", "overdue!". Debt going down is the story.
Privacy as a through-line. Calmly remind that data is yours and stays local — "Nothing leaves your network."
Numbers. No cents in summaries ($18,240); cents only in ledgers. Frame time positively.
No emoji, ever. Status is carried by color + icon + word — nothing else.

Foundations

An 8px rhythm on a 4px base. Soft-but-not-pill radii. Quiet, cool-toned shadows — depth from surface contrast, not weight. Motion is calm and quick, with no bounce.
